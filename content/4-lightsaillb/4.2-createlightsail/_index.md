#### Create Lightsail instance from snapshot


1. Once the snapshot has been created, click on the 3 dots icon.

![Lightsail](/images/8/0001.png?featherlight=false&width=90pc)

1. Click **Create new instance** to proceed to create Lightsail instance from snapshot.

![Lightsail](/images/8/0002.png?featherlight=false&width=90pc)

2. Click **Change zone** to change the Availability Zone for the Lightsail instance, we will create 2 new Lightsail instances in 2 different Availability zones from the original Lightsail instance.

![Lightsail](/images/8/0003.png?featherlight=false&width=90pc)

3. Click **Zone B**.

![Lightsail](/images/8/0004.png?featherlight=false&width=90pc)

4. Scroll to the bottom of the screen, name the Lightsail instance **PHP-FE-2** and click **Create instance**.

![Lightsail](/images/8/0005.png?featherlight=false&width=90pc)
