1. **PrestaShop is an easy-to-use open-source e-commerce platform** that allows users to create online stores and integrate them with their preferred payment gateways, WordPress, accounting software (such as Akaunting), and various other applications. This can be a self-managed alternative to Shopify or Etsy, for example.

   - Deploy the Blueprint
   - Click on the Home button in the Lightsail banner at the top to return to the 'Instances' page. Click "Create instance."

   ![Create VPC](/images/2/0001.png?featherlight=false&width=90pc)

2. Scroll down and **choose PrestaShop as the template**.

   - Scroll down and **choose PrestaShop as the template**.
   - **Select a plan for your instance**. Choose the $5 per month instance with 1GB of memory and 1 vCPU.

   ![Create VPC](/images/2/0002.png?featherlight=false&width=90pc)

3. **Name the virtual machine 'Workshop-PrestaShop'**.
   - Click **Create instance** to proceed.

   ![Create VPC](/images/2/0003.png?featherlight=false&width=90pc)
