1. Now, we will proceed to the third application, which is also our last application, called 'Akaunting,' an open-source financial management application. Akaunting has plugins that integrate with both WordPress e-commerce and PrestaShop. These are paid plugins, so we won't include them in this session. Remember that this software can indeed integrate with other tools for small businesses.

- Click on the Home button at the top of the Lightsail dashboard and then click on **Create instance**.

![Create VPC](/images/3/0001.png?featherlight=false&width=90pc)

2. Scroll down to the 'Select a blueprint' section and click on OS Only, then choose Ubuntu 20.04 LTS as the template you want to use.

![Create VPC](/images/3/0002.png?featherlight=false&width=90pc)

3. Choose your virtual machine package
- Select the '$5 USD' package with 1GB of memory and 1 vCPU.

![Create VPC](/images/3/0003.png?featherlight=false&width=90pc)

4. Name your virtual machine 'Workshop-Akaunting' and then click **Create instance**.

![Create VPC](/images/3/0004.png?featherlight=false&width=90pc)
